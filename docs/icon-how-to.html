How to Use the LCARS Icon Generator

The icon generator is privacy-first. It does not scan your phone, read your installed apps, require ADB, require Termux, or upload your app list.

Recommended method

Type or paste app names

The easiest method is to write one app name per line. This works on Android, iPhone, iPad, desktop, and basically anything else. 

Discord
Brave
ChatGPT
Photos
Music
Calendar

After parsing, the generator guesses categories. You can edit every category before downloading icons.

Optional upload method

Upload a list you already have

If you already have an app list from your launcher, backup tool, notes app, spreadsheet, or another trusted method, you can upload it.

The generator accepts app names:

Discord
Brave
ChatGPT

It also accepts package names, mostly useful for Android users:

com.discord
com.brave.browser
com.openai.chatgpt

And mixed CSV-style rows:

Discord,com.discord
Brave,com.brave.browser
ChatGPT,com.openai.chatgpt

Android

App names are enough

For simple generated icon files, app names are fine. Package names are more precise for Android launcher/icon-pack mapping, but they are optional.

This site does not ask you to connect your phone to a computer, install a terminal app, or give another app permission to inspect your device.

Privacy note

Your app list can reveal personal habits. The generator runs locally in your browser, and unknown-app contribution JSON is copied only if you choose to copy it.
